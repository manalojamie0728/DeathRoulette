-- CHARACTERS --

* Ichirou Yokohama (Red Motif) - The Upholder of Justice
* Akira Ichibana (Black Motif) - The Comic Relief
* Sumiko Tokubei (Grey Motif) - The Intelligent Yet Forgetful
* Inoue Shinozaki (Pink Motif) - The Curious Cat
* Sayo Ronoroa (Purple Motif) - The Leader and Most Responsible; the student council president and suspected to be the culprit for the killings. Graduates as a Top 5 student in their batch. "Besides, what interest is there?"
* Yoshiro Suzuki (Yellow Motif) - The Puzzle Geek
* Hiroshi Kano (Brown Motif) - The Silent Entertainer
* Miyu Hirano (Violet/Green Motif) - The Cynical-Optimistic Detective/The Chessmaster; one of the students from IV-C and shows a lot of interest in mathematics and mystery-solving. He is actually the main antagonist and the origin of "Let". Graduates as a Top 8 student in their batch.
* Kyou Kirisaki (Orange Motif) - The Pious
* Hikaru Yamamoto (Blue Motif) - The Resident Prankster
* Lilith/ Lawlet "Let" Carnegie - a manifestation of the culprit of the Death Roulette Curse Killings. Ambiguously male or female, because his/her gender is subject to whoever sees him/her. Is fond of nursery rhymes and violent games. The Death Roulette is his/her favorite, hence the moniker.
* Ikuko Mimori - Sayo's classmate and fellow churchmate at the Youth Group. She occasionally gives Sayo useful advice which plays a vital role in the latter's subplot in the October chapter.
* Sanae Yoshida - Miyu's close friend and love interest. They met each other at a high school before Miyu transferred to Maria St. Claire.
* Aria Utada - Hikaru's best friend and one of the most popular singers in their batch.
